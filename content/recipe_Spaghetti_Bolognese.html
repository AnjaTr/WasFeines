Vegan Lentil Bolognese

20-25min, Lazy Dinner

Just 10 main ingredients, should not take longer than 20-25min.

Apart from normal pantry ingredients, just make sure you have walnuts, red lentils, and red wine.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery stick
  • 1/2 cup of walnuts
  • 1/2 cup of red lentils
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp of oregano
  • 1 tsp of basil
  • 1 tsp of thyme
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp of pepper
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of red wine
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Chop the onion, garlic, carrot, and celery.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a pan and add the onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Cook for 5min.
  3. Add the walnuts and cook for another 5min.
  4. Add the red lentils, tomatoes, tomato paste, soy sauce, balsamic vinegar, oregano, basil, thyme, salt, pepper, water, and red wine.
  5. Simmer for 10min.
  6. Enjoy with pasta or rice.
